Frequently Asked Questions

Does rain affect the service?

We know Melbourne weather! Rain is actually pure; it's dirt that makes windows muddy. If it rains within 48 hours and your windows are dirty again, Bolto's will reclean them for free. No stress.

How do you estimate price?

Our online calculator provides a close estimate based on standard house sizes. We double-check the property size via Google Earth before booking. If you have colonial bars (French panes) or paint on the glass, we'll adjust the quote onsite before starting.
