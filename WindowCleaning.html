Frequently Asked Questions

Does rain affect the service?

We know Melbourne weather is unpredictable! Rain water is actually very pure. It's the dust on the windows that turns to mud when it rains. If it rains during your clean or 48 hours after, and the windows are dirty again, we will reclean them for free.

How do you estimate price?

Our online calculator is a guide based on average house sizes. We use Google Earth to verify the size of the property before confirming your booking. If there are French Panes (colonial bars) or paint removal required, we will adjust the quote onsite before starting.
