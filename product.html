Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • What types of images work best?

    High-resolution images (300 DPI or more) in JPG or PNG format provide the best results. Vector files (.AI, .EPS) can also be submitted via email for even sharper prints. Avoid low-resolution images as they may appear pixelated when enlarged.

  • Can I order bulk designs for a team or company?

    Absolutely! We offer discounts for bulk orders. Please contact our sales team for a custom quote.

  • What’s the turnaround time?

    Standard turnaround time is 7-10 business days after design approval and payment, plus shipping. This can vary based on component availability and order complexity.

  • Are the designs permanent? Do they fade?

    Our UV printing process creates a highly durable and scratch-resistant finish. The prints are designed to last for years without fading under normal indoor conditions.
