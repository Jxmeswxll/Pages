Detailed Design & Customization Process

Unleash your creativity! Here's how to personalize your PC case in detail:

  1. Upload Your Design: We accept JPG and PNG files. For best results, use high-resolution images (see guidelines below). The uploader is in the left panel.
  2. Choose Placement: Specify where you want your design:
    • Full Side Panel (Glass or Solid)
    • Front Panel
    • Top Panel (selected cases)
    • Multiple Panels for a wrap-around effect (Contact us for this option)
  3. Preview Your Masterpiece: Once uploaded (left panel), you'll see a mockup of your design on the selected PC case. (Note: Interactive 3D preview coming soon!)

Optional Add-ons (Select in Full Configurator):

  • RGB Lighting Sync: Synchronize internal and external RGB lighting with your design's color palette.
  • Custom Etching: Add subtle text or logos etched into glass panels.

Full Upload Instructions & Guidelines

  • Supported Formats: JPG, PNG.
  • Maximum File Size: 10MB.
  • Recommended Resolution: Minimum 300 DPI for the intended print size. For a full side panel (e.g., 40cm x 40cm), aim for at least 4700x4700 pixels.
  • Aspect Ratio: Ensure your image fits the panel dimensions or be prepared for potential cropping. We'll provide panel templates soon.
  • Content Restrictions: No copyrighted material (unless you own the rights), hate speech, explicit, or illegal content. We reserve the right to refuse any design.
