Frequently Asked Questions

How does shipping work for online bundle purchases?

Online bundle orders placed before October 15th will aim to ship before the event starts. Orders placed after this date or during the event will ship starting the week of October 21st. Standard Australian shipping times apply. Express options are available at checkout.

What is the warranty on the bundles?

All Aftershock PCs included in the bundles come with our standard 3-Year Pickup & Return Warranty. Peripherals (monitors, keyboards, mice, headsets) are covered by their respective manufacturer warranties (Samsung, SteelSeries). Please retain proof of purchase.

Can I buy the bundles at the IEM event?

Yes! A limited quantity of the 'AORUS + NVIDIA Powered Gaming Bundle' will be available for purchase directly at our booth (#A12) during IEM Melbourne. These are first come, first served. The Giveaway Bundle is entry-only.

How do I enter the Premium Giveaway Bundle?

You can enter the giveaway via the link on this page (Enter Giveaway) which will become active closer to the event, or by visiting our booth (#A12) at IEM Melbourne and scanning the QR code. One entry per person. Full terms and conditions will be available on the entry page.

Who do I contact for technical support?

For technical support regarding the Aftershock PC itself, please visit our support page or call us at [Your Phone Number]. For issues with peripherals (monitor, keyboard, mouse, headset), please contact Samsung or SteelSeries support directly through their respective websites.
