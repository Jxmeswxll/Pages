Need Answers?

How does shipping work for online bundle purchases?

Online bundle orders placed before October 15th, 2024 will aim to ship *before* the event. Orders placed during or after this date will commence shipping the week of October 21st. Standard Australian shipping timelines apply. Express options are available at checkout.

What warranty comes with the bundles?

All Aftershock PCs within the bundles include our standard 3-Year Pickup & Return Warranty. Peripherals (monitors, keyboards, etc.) are covered by their respective manufacturer warranties (e.g., Samsung, SteelSeries). Keep your proof of purchase safe!

Can I buy the 'IEM Victory Bundle' at the event?

Yes! A limited quantity of the 'IEM Victory Bundle' (RTS 1 System + Peripherals) will be available for direct purchase at our Booth #A12 during IEM Melbourne. These are strictly first come, first served. Arrive early!
